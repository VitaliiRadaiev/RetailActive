OUR SERVICES

Perfect Store

Understand how your shoppers perceive your brand and your competitor’s brands in store and online. Our perfect store scoring methodology attributes scoring to brand presence and promotion highlighting opportunities to improve as well as reward.

Compliance

Audit your locations to check compliance against brand and franchise standards, processes, industry accreditations and legal requirements. Using our specialist team to audit sites or in house teams using our system, our reporting identifies issues and produces action plans.

Brand Standards

Using our team of industry experts to audit compliance to brand or franchise standards and processes. Can also be deployed as self audits for use by in-house teams .

Knowledge Bank

Consolidate all of your data into one place to save time and drive efficiency. This solution provides one drillable location for ease of use and the ability to link and compare sources.

Mystery Shopping

Provides a consistent measure of customer experience through enactment of scenarios. Can compare experience between regions and retailers as well as in store, online and through home delivery.

Satisfaction Surveys

Understand satisfaction vs expectation with your products or services. Gather positive and negative perceptions to improve your marketing efforts and strategy. Keep a pulse on how your customers or your employees are feeling and make improvements to drive sales, loyalty and reputation.

Engagement Surveys

Understand how your teams feel about working for your business. Quickly identify issues and improvements to increase engagement and loyalty.

Exit Survey and Focus Groups

Speak directly to customers and non-customers to gain qualitative data and in depth insights. Learn about their needs and perspectives on your brand as well as your competitior’s.
